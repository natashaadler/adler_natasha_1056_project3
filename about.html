Our Story

Founded more than 165 years ago, Anheuser-Busch is one of America’s most iconic companies and the undisputed leader of the U.S. beer industry. We are proud of our history and heritage and remain committed to brewing the great-tasting, high-quality beers that have satisfied beer drinkers for generations. Today, we are 18,000 employees colleagues strong—spread out across the country, united by a deep passion for making great beer and an unwavering commitment to supporting the communities that we call home. We want to share with you our pride in what we do, who we are, and what our vision is for the future of our company – this is what gives us purpose beyond brewing.
